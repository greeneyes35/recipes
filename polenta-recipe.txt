Ingredients:
-  315 mg polenta
- 1,5 L water
- 150 ml milk
- 75 g butter
- 1,5 salt

Directions:
1)  Bring the water to boiling temperature and turn down the temperature
2)  Add little by little polenta  to the water, while stirring with a spoon
3)  When it becomes more firm add the milk, butter and salt
4)  Keep stirring until it has the right consistency
